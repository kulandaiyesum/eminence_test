FAQs

You have questions. We have answers.

How do I know the content is accurate?

While AI chatbots such as ChatGPT will fail to produce accurate content or citations, Eminence uses a framework that combines LLMs with external data to ensure high-quality questions and explanations. The external data used in our algorithm consists of verified exam prep content.

What exams are currently offered?

We currently offer exam prep content for the USMLE Step 1 and 2, as well as board certification exams for residents/physicians. As EminenceAI grows our user base, we will look to expand outside of medicine.

I am a resident/physician. Can I request content for a board certification exam?

Yes! If you are a resident/physician preparing for a board certification exam that you do not see on our website, we take requests! Send an email to sj@eminenceai.com with the type of exam and we will make it happen.
